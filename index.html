What works: pure-Python packages from PyPI — scikit-learn, scipy, matplotlib, requests, statsmodels, sympy, networkx...

What doesn't: packages with native C extensions not compiled for WebAssembly (e.g. lightgbm, xgboost). Most popular data science libraries work fine.
